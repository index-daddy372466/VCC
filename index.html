What can a career counselor do for me?

+

I’ll help you clarify your goals, explore career options, and develop a practical action plan to achieve your goals. I’m here to help you navigate transitions while you build confidence and create value in your life and in others’.

Do you assist Service members and/or Veterans?

+

Absolutely. I have worked in non-profits for persons with disabilities as well as within the government, assisting Service members and Veterans with and without disabilities.

Can I afford career counseling?

+

Yes! And the number of sessions depends on each person's needs. It can be as few as 2 or as many as 20 sessions. I am here with you all the way.
