## Role and responsibilities

1. Assist individuals with disabilities in setting and achieving personal, social, and career goals. 
1. Conduct comprehensive assessments of a client's abilities, skills, and vocational interests. 
1. Develop and coordinate personalized rehabilitation plans to support self-sufficiency and integration into the community. 
1. Collaborate with employers to create inclusive work environments. 
1. Provide counseling and mental health support. 
1. Coordinate with other service providers and advocate for clients' needs.
